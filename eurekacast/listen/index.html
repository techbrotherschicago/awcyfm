EurekaCast NOW! is broadcasted live every Saturday, 8 to 9 pm CST, on WLPN-LP 105.5 FM Lumpen Radio. You can also catch rebroadcasts on Mondays from 8 to 9 pm CST on WIIT 88.9 FM Chicago.
